Journey Through Time

7500 BCE

First settled communities in Jordan, including 'Ain Ghazal, one of the largest known prehistoric settlements.

312 BCE

Petra becomes the capital of the Nabataean Kingdom, flourishing as a trade center.

106 CE

Roman annexation and construction of major cities including Jerash and Philadelphia (modern Amman).

636 CE

Islamic era begins, bringing new cultural and architectural influences.

1946

Jordan gains independence and establishes the Hashemite Kingdom.
